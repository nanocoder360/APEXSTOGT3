04 — GT3 Class Compliance

BUILT FOR
THE GRID

GT3 is homologation-based, performance-equalised by BoP. The Apex STO is engineered to compete within this regulated envelope — not above it.

⚡
BoP Power Control
Peak output managed within the 400–500 kW GT3 EV window. Energy per lap is FIA-regulated, preventing any single manufacturer from gaining advantage outside the BoP framework.
🔋
Battery Safety Architecture
Mandatory liquid and phase-change thermal cooling. Armored crash enclosure with automatic high-voltage shutdown and fire containment foam. Non-negotiable for EV racing homologation.
🏁
Aerodynamics
Fixed rear wing, adjustable angle only. Homologated splitter and flat-floor diffuser. No active aero for performance gain. EV classification grants zero additional aero freedom versus ICE GT3.
✓ Allowed Systems
Regenerative braking (axle-limited)
Controlled energy deployment maps
Fast charging pit system
Battery thermal management AI
Traction and stability controls (driver levels)
Telemetry and race engineer assistance
⚠ Restricted / Excluded
Autonomous steering or throttle control
Real-time AI racing line execution
Active aero generating performance gain
Performance-relevant airflow turbines
Any system altering lap time outside BoP
05 — Artificial Intelligence Systems

SMARTER
EVERY LAP

🎯
Real-Time Hazard Detection
Continuous sensor fusion monitors track conditions, proximity hazards, and environmental changes for instant safety responses.
🔥
Battery & Thermal Monitoring
AI tracks cell temperature, charge state, and thermal runaway risk in real time — adjusting cooling maps dynamically per track sector.
📡
Performance Deployment
Dynamic power maps optimize energy use across each lap, preserving charge for critical overtaking zones without sacrificing pace.
🌦
Environmental Adaptation
Detects rain, fog, sandstorms, and extreme heat — automatically adjusting traction parameters, visibility systems, and battery insulation protection.

DRIVER ALWAYS IN CONTROL

GT3 regulations are clear: the driver must fully control the vehicle at all times. The Apex STO's AI operates purely as a support layer — monitoring, advising, and protecting — never executing steering or throttle inputs.

What AI Cannot Do in GT3

Autonomous driving, AI steering/throttle override, and real-time lap execution are categorically excluded. These are not design choices — they are FIA mandates for the GT3 class.
